KiddoLog Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2025 Applies to the KiddoLog mobile application

KiddoLog ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address and password for authentication
  • Baby Profile Data: Baby's name, birth date, gender, blood type, and photo (optional)

Health & Activity Data

  • Feeding records (breast, bottle, solid food)
  • Sleep patterns and duration
  • Diaper change logs
  • Growth measurements (weight, height, head circumference)
  • Health records (symptoms, medications, vaccinations, doctor visits)
  • Allergy information

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • Push notification tokens (if enabled)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To sync your data across devices
  • To send push notifications (reminders, alerts)
  • To improve our services

Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored securely on Supabase servers
  • Data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • Your data is associated with your account and not shared with third parties

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes

We may share data only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

Family Sharing Feature

KiddoLog allows you to share baby profiles with family members using a share code. Shared members can view and add records. You control who has access.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data through the app
  • Update your information at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data (feature available in app)

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through:

  • Go to Profile → Delete Account
  • Confirm deletion

This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.

Children's Privacy

KiddoLog is designed to help parents track their children's development. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children under 13.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • Apple Push Notification Service: For notifications
  • RevenueCat/StoreKit: For subscription management

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
